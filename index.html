About Me

Jerold Williams is a soulful and strategic Creative Project Manager with over a decade of experience leading cross-functional teams in advertising, marketing, and public health campaigns. With a firm handshake and an eye for excellence, Jerold brings structure, insight, and style to every engagement—from Fortune 100 brands to federal agencies and nonprofit powerhouses.

His production leadership has helped launch national campaigns for AARP Foundation, the CDC, Capital One, and the U.S. Department of Education. Whether he’s overseeing a fast-moving video shoot, managing multimillion-dollar budgets, or guiding branding efforts from ideation to delivery, Jerold blends clarity, creativity, and calm under pressure.

Known for his respect-driven leadership and love for community-centered storytelling, Jerold’s work doesn’t just hit deadlines—it resonates.
